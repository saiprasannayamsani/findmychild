🔐 3-Level Verification System

Every report is verified through three layers to prevent misuse and ensure trustworthy data

📧

Level 1 — Email Login

Register and login with email. All complaints are linked to a verified account. No anonymous filing.

📄

Level 2 — Birth Certificate

Upload birth certificate. Registration number verified against govt CRS database. Proves family relationship.

🔐

Level 3 — Aadhaar OTP

Both missing person's and reporter's Aadhaar required. OTP sent to Aadhaar-linked mobile for final verification.

🤝

AI Matching

AI automatically matches missing and found reports by age, gender and location in milliseconds.

📡

Auto Escalation

24hrs→District. 48hrs→State. 72hrs→NCRB National. Fully automatic with SMS alerts at each stage.

👮

Police Verification

Physical ID + original documents verified at police station before reunion. Final security layer.
